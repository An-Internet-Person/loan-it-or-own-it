Loan It or Own It

A swipeable thought experiment about personal ownership vs community access. Choose between the world as it is—and the world as it could be.

How to Play

In Loan It or Own It, you’ll be presented with a series of “Would You Rather…” choices. Each one compares two ways of living:

  • Owning something for yourself
  • Accessing something shared by all

Choose the option that makes the most sense to you. Then you’ll see the pros and cons of both sides. There’s no right answer—just thoughtful questions.

Wait, what do you mean by “loan it”?

Think of a public library: you don’t own the book, but you have full access when you need it—and you return it when you’re done. In legal terms, this is called usufruct: the right to use and enjoy something without owning it. It’s a fancy word for a pretty old idea.

What’s Next

Future versions of Loan It or Own It will let you:

  • Create an account and track your choices across sessions
  • Compare your Alt World with friends
  • See global stats—like how many people chose access over ownership
  • Contribute your own scenarios to the game

For now, your score is saved locally. But this is just the beginning.
